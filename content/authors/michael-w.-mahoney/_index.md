Michael Mahoney is in the Department of Statistics at UC Berkeley. He is also at the International Computer Science Institute (ICSI, where he is Vice President and Director of the Big Data Group) and the Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory (LBNL, where he is the Group Lead for the Machine Learning and Analytics Group). He is also in the RISELab (in the past AMPLab) in the Department of EECS, and he is an Amazon Scholar.
